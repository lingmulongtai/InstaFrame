Allow online location services?

Your photos and videos are never uploaded. If you continue, location data may be sent to the services below.

  • Nominatim: latitude/longitude for place-name lookup
  • OpenStreetMap: map tiles; ipapi only if device location is unavailable
  • Mapbox: latitude/longitude when generating a map overlay

Choose access for this browser session or remember the choice on this device. You can revoke it from Customize.
